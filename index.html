Logo Design

These logo designs were made for my photogrpahy site. My style is inspired by landscapes and human connection. The tree symbolizes the outdoors while the circle with my business name represents connection and loyalty. I wanted the logo to have a free and flowing feel to it which is why I decided on a more whimsical tree rather than a geometric one.
